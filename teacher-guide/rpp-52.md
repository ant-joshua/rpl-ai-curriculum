## Pertemuan 1: Freelance Hustle — Platform, Profil, Pricing, Client Communication, Tax & Legal

### Tujuan
- Memilih platform freelance sesuai skill & target pasar (Sribu, Fastwork, Upwork)
- Membuat profil freelance profesional (foto, headline, bio, portfolio)
- Menentukan pricing kompetitif dengan rate calculator
- Berkomunikasi dengan klien secara profesional (brief, proposal, revisi, payment)
- Memahami aspek pajak & legal dasar sebagai freelancer Indonesia

### Kegiatan (90 menit)
| Waktu | Aktivitas | Metode | Media |
|-------|-----------|--------|-------|
| 10' | Apersepsi & review: tren kerja freelance di Indonesia, demo profil freelance vs karyawan | Tanya jawab | Slide, browser (Sribu, Upwork) |
| 20' | Materi inti: platform lokal vs global, komponen profil, model pricing (fixed/hourly/retainer), rate calculator, client communication stages, PPh Final 0.5%, NPWP, invoice | Ceramah + demo | Live code (rate calc), slide |
| 25' | Praktik terbimbing: buat draft profil freelance + hitung rate pake calculator | Hands-on | Starter template spreadsheet |
| 20' | Latihan mandiri: roleplay client communication (brief → proposal → revisi → payment), siapkan invoice | Problem solving | Soal, roleplay sheet |
| 15' | Diskusi & refleksi: tantangan freelancer Indonesia, strategi dapat project pertama | Q&A | — |
