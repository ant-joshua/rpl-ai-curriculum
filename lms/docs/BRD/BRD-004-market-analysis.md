## 4. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Tutor Virality (P6)
- Free for private tutors (no subscription fee)
- Referral program: invite 5 tutors = free month of premium
- Distribution: Facebook groups "Guru Les Privat Indonesia" (500K+ members)

### Phase 2: Bimbel Pilot (P6+)
- 3 bimbel as beta testers (free 6 months for feedback)
- Case study: "Bimbel Pintar cut admin time 80%"
- Pricing: intro Rp500K/mo flat for first 10 clients

### Phase 3: School Partnerships (P2-P5)
- Start with 1 school (SMA Kembang or similar)
- Academic year cycle: onboard before July (new academic year)
- Pricing: Rp1M/mo first year, Rp2M/mo renewal

### Phase 4: University (P7)
- Deferred to Year 2
- Pricing: Rp2-5M/mo

## 5. Key Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Schools prefer paper-based | Medium | High | Demo rapor PDF quality, show time savings |
| Tutors price-sensitive (won't pay) | High | Medium | Make free tier generous, upsell premium features |
| Bimbel prefer existing SIAKAD | Low | Medium | Target bimbel without any system (majority) |
| D1 100-table limit | Low | Medium | Monitor, consolidate rarely-used tables |
